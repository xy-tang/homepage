General

Dr. Tang received the Ph.D. from the department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in Jan. 2016, supervised by Prof. Angela Yingjun Zhang. After that, she has been a postdoc/scientist at EPFL and CUHK from 2016 to 2019, respectively. Dr. Tang has a previous name: Wanrong Tang.

Dr. Tang will join the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong(Shenzhen) as a Tenure Track Assistant Professor in Feb. 2020.

Research Interests

1)Online scheduling and distributed algorithm design and optimization in network systems, e.g., smart grid, intelligent transportation network, mobile edge computing.

2)Fundamental research in optimization theory, machine learning and theoretical computer science.
